Melody Williams holds a Master of Arts in Art Education and a BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Their teaching practice and their work in fashion, fiber arts, and printmedia connect somatic experience and individualized, process-based learning to anti-oppression arts education. In Chicago they have participated via teaching and leadership roles in community-level arts programs across the city, including Project Onward at the Chicago Cultural Center, archiTreasures, Old Towne School of Folk Music, and SAIC at Homan Square. Currently, they are an active member of Spaces for Possibility, a collaborative studio project for K-12 art teachers.
